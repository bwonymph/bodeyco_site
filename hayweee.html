Hayley Buchholz


@hayweee

Hayley Buchholz is a Manhattan based multimedia artist with a graphic design and videography background. She has a full-time job as an Art Director and has worked on clients ranging from Crown Royal, Hugo Boss, Hyatt Hotels and, most recently, Xfinity. She uses photography and graphic collage as a personal outlet to express her life experiences. In her studio, you will find a coming-of-age story translated into art. She hopes that through BODEYCO, her work can inspire joie de vivre in the hearts and homes of others.
